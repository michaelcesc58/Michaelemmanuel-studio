Executive Summary
Why Vellora, why now.

Nigeria's premium beverage market is growing at 11% annually, driven by an expanding upper-middle class and a cultural shift toward curated dining. Vellora enters with a differentiated positioning: an accessible premium wine designed for the Nigerian palate, occasion, and social identity.

Campaign Roadmap
Four phases. One story arc.
01
Foundation & Brand Launch
Months 1–2

Establish Vellora's identity across all three cities. Seed awareness through press, influencer partnerships, and trade introductions.

  • Brand launch events in Abuja and Lagos (private media dinners)
  • Distribute to 30 premium restaurants and hotels across 3 cities
  • Activate 15 tier-1 lifestyle influencers (Instagram + TikTok)
  • PR placements in BusinessDay, Vanguard Allure, ThisDay Style
02
Market Penetration
Months 3–5

Scale through paid media and experiential activations. Drive trial through retail and branded events.

  • Paid social campaign targeting income-verified Nigerians
  • "Vellora Evening" pop-up tasting events in all 3 cities
  • Retail listing in ShopRite Premium, Spar, fine wine boutiques
  • Corporate gifting programme for C-suite decision makers
03
Investor Visibility & Conversion
Months 6–9

Host structured investor roundtables and launch the Cellar Club loyalty programme.

  • Vellora Investor Roundtable Series — 3 cities, quarterly
  • Launch Cellar Club membership (exclusive allocations + updates)
  • LinkedIn B2B campaign targeting CFOs and investment directors
  • Vellora Reserve Edition — limited allocation creates scarcity
04
Retention & Scale
Months 10–12

Consolidate market share, deepen loyalty, and build the case for national rollout.

  • "Vellora Harvest Gala" — Lagos flagship event for 300 HNIs
  • Year 1 investor impact report — performance, community, growth
  • Introduce subscription model (6-bottle monthly programme)
  • National rollout planning: Port Harcourt, Enugu
